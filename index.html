Senitron RFID Inventory Tracking

User Experience and Interaction Design, Ruby on Rails applications

Senitron is a leading developer of radio frequency identification hardware and software with a focus on inventory visibility and tracking. They provide continuous real-time monitoring of RFID tags (attached to inventory items) in dense populations (100,000+ tags). Using a network of RFID tag-reading antennas, inventory is tracked with over 99% accuracy in real time.

Covisus Quantitative Optical Analysis

User Experience and Interaction Design, 3D Design & Development

Covisus, through its DTEK technology, can track and determine conformance of physical items (such as computer chips) to original manufacturing condition without the use of external tags. Material surfaces have a randomized structure at a microscopic scale. Like a fingerprint or a snow flake, these patterns are unique and intrinsic to each individual item and cannot be predicted or replicated. The DTEK system uses algorithms to compare parts against a golden sample and determine product quality conformance.
