Intuitive Surgical Warehouse Inventory Management

The Senitron RFID tag tracking system delivers a hands-free solution for tracking items. This allows staff to stay on top of inventory levels 24/7 with extremely high accuracy. After installing hundreds of RFID tracking antennas at one of Intuitive's San Jose warehouses, a back-end developer and myself implemented a custom software application to track inventory for their da Vinci surgical robot system. I designed and developed the user interface and experience. The 200,000 square feet warehouse enables asset tracking with excellent granularity down to particular shelves, aisles, and shelf-levels.
