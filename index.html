Senitron RFID Inventory Tracking

User Experience and Interaction Design

Senitron develops RFID (radio frequency identification) software and hardware to track inventory with over 99% accuracy in real time. They provide continuous real-time monitoring of RFID tags (attached to inventory items) in dense populations (in some cases 100,000+ tags). Using a network of tag-reading antennas and our custom built software applications, we were able to achieve highly accurate inventory visibility. I was responsible for translating client requirements into an easy to use web interface.

Covisus Quantitative Optical Analysis

User Experience and Interaction Design, 3D Design & Development

Material surfaces have a randomized structure at a microscopic scale. Like a fingerprint, these patterns are unique and intrinsic to each individual item and cannot be predicted or replicated. Covisus, through its DTEK technology, can track and determine the conformance of physical items to original manufacturing condition without the use of external tags. The DTEK system uses algorithms to compare parts against a golden sample and determine product quality conformance. My task was to develop a user interface for taking in information about a specific component, and then displaying it in a report based on the results.
