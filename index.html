Covisus Quantitative Optical Analysis

3D Design & Development - UI/UX - HTML, CSS - Bootstrap

Covisus, through its proprietary DTEK technology, can track and determine conformance of physical items to original manufacturing condition without the use of external tags. Material surfaces have a randomized structure at the micrometer and nanometer scale. Like a fingerprint, an iris scan, or a snow flake, these patterns are unique and intrinsic to each individual item and cannot be predicted or replicated.
The DTEK optically analyzes the surface of an electronic component and outputs unambiguous quantitative information about the surface for the purpose of comparative analysis.
A web application was created to upload and analyze surface texture scans and then determine product conformance and authenticity by comparing to a golden part. Above is the report generated by each scan set.
