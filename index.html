2014   Senitron RFID Inventory Tracking

UI/UX (HTML, SCSS, & jQuery) in Ruby/Rails framework - PostgreSQL - Foundation framework

Senitron is a leading developer of Radio Frequency IDentification hardware and software with a focus on inventory visibility and tracking. They provide continuous real-time monitoring of RFID tags (attached to inventory items) in dense populations (100,000+ tags). Using a network of RFID readers and antennas, tagged inventory is tracked with close to 100% accuracy in real time. 3D Design & Development - UI/UX - HTML, CSS, & jQuery in Ruby/Rails framework - Bootstrap framework

Covisus, through its proprietary DTEK technology, can track and determine conformance of physical items to original manufacturing condition without the use of external tags. Material surfaces have a randomized structure at the micrometer and nanometer scale. Like a fingerprint, an iris scan, or a snow flake, these patterns are unique and intrinsic to each individual item and cannot be predicted or replicated
